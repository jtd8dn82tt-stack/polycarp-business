About Us

Polycarp Enterprise is a professional ICT, Cyber Café and Training Services company based in Nairobi, Kenya. We provide computer repair and maintenance, software installation, networking solutions, website development, cyber café services, printing, photocopying, scanning, KRA services, eCitizen services, HELB applications and professional ICT training. Our mission is to provide reliable, affordable and innovative technology solutions while promoting digital literacy and supporting business growth.
